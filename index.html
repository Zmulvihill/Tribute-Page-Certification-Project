Bill Watterson

The man who created Calvin and Hobbes

Here's a time line of Bill's work:

  • 1980 - Hired on trial basis at the Cincinatti Post.
  • 1981 - Gets fired from Cincinatti Post and gets hired at a small advertising agency creating grocery ads while on the side designing his own cartoons.
  • 1984 - Does freelance work as an artist for his brother's band, calendars, clothing graphics, educational books, magazine covers, posters, and post cards.
  • 1985 - Calvin and Hobbes is first published, the first series is called Auf Dem Sprung.
  • 1988 - Something under the bed is drooling is published.
  • 1989 - Yukon Ho, A Calvin and Hobbes collection is published.
  • 1990 - Weirdos from another planet is published.
  • 1991 - Revenge of the baby sat is published.
  • 1992 - Attack of the Deranged Mutant Killer Monster Snow Goons is published.
  • 1993 - Days are just packed Calvin and Hobbes is published.
  • 1994 - Homicidal Psycho Jungle Cat is published.
  • 1996 - There's treasure everywhere is published.
  • 1996 - It's a magical world: A Calvin and Hobbes Collection is published.
  • 2001 - Calvin and Hobbes: Sunday Pages is published.
  • 2005 - The Complete Calvin and Hobbes hardcover is published.
  • 2015 - Exploring Calvin and Hobbes: An Exhibition Catalogue is published.
